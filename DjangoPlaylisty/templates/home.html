Creating a playlist on our web app is a breeze! We offer you a variety of options to curate your perfect Spotify playlist. Whether you prefer selecting artists, albums, or individual songs, we've got you covered.

Why do you have to log in?

To be able to generate Spotify playlists using our web app, we kindly ask you to log in with your Spotify account. This is a necessary step to provide you with a seamless and personalized experience. We understand that you may have concerns about privacy and the security of your information, so we want to assure you that your data is safe.

When you log in with your Spotify account, our web app uses Spotify's Application Programming Interface (API) to securely authenticate your account. The API allows us to access limited information about your Spotify profile, such as your display name and public playlists, which we use solely for the purpose of generating personalized playlists for you.

It's important to note that we do not store or have access to your Spotify account credentials, such as your username or password. The authentication process is handled entirely by Spotify, ensuring that your sensitive information remains confidential. We prioritize your privacy and adhere to all necessary security measures to protect your data.

By logging in with your Spotify account, you enable our web app to leverage the vast music library and features offered by Spotify. This allows us to curate playlists tailored to your musical preferences, considering your listening history, favorite artists, and genre preferences. The result is a personalized playlist experience that caters to your unique taste in music.

We value your trust and understand the importance of transparency when it comes to handling user data. Rest assured that we abide by Spotify's terms of service and privacy policy, and we take all necessary precautions to ensure your privacy and security throughout your interaction with our web app.
